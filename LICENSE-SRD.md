# System Reference Document 5.1 attribution

The Storyteller Toolbox D&D 5e character builder — the class, race, background,
levelling, and spell-slot rules in `v2/src/engine/dnd5e.ts` and
`v2/src/composites/dnd-character.ts` — includes material derived from the
**System Reference Document 5.1 ("SRD 5.1")**.

> This work includes material taken from the System Reference Document 5.1
> ("SRD 5.1") by Wizards of the Coast LLC and available at
> https://dnd.wizards.com/resources/systems-reference-document. The SRD 5.1 is
> licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License
> available at https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode.

Only SRD 5.1 content is used. Background names other than "Acolyte" (the SRD's
background) are generic skill archetypes of our own naming, not Player's
Handbook backgrounds. No Product Identity is included.

This attribution is separate from the community-table `credits` (r/d100,
r/BehindTheTables, DnDSpeak, etc.) surfaced on the site's About page.
